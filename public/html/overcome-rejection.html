Overcoming the fear of rejection


Is your fear of getting rejected interfering with your life?. Well you are not alone, there are many people who just won't get themselves in any situation where they'll experience rejection. The bad news is, life will throw rocks at you, so you need to get over your fear of rejection ASAP, so that you can leave your life the way you want, and not give a damn whenever you get rejected at something.

Below are some of the steps you can take to overcome your fear of rejection.



ADMIT IT


Admitting that you are struggling with something is the first step to destroy it head-on. By admitting that you are afraid to get rejected let’s your mind know that this is something interfering with your life and it needs to be dealt with immediately.



Source: Makeameme.org



GET REJECTED


After admitting that you are afraid of getting rejected, the next step is to get rejected as many times as you can. Just like other phobias like the fear of spiders, you need to face your fears so that eventually your mind is not afraid of that fear again. When you consistently put yourself in any uncomfortable situation, your mind starts to relax every time you do that thing and your mind won’t activate itself to the SAFE mode anymore or activate to FIGHT and FLIGHT mode.



Source: Makeameme.org



FOLLOW RULES


When getting yourself out there to get rejected, you need to consider the following rules in order to actually benefit from this.



Source: Makeameme.org


Rule 1

Start small, don’t just go to your boss and ask for a raise, start by getting rejected on small things that won’t hurt your feelings too much.


Rule 2

Put yourself in situations where you know or you are likely to get rejected. Example, ask the cashier at the store to give you a discount on the things you bought. If you your proposal gets approved or you don’t get rejected, that doesn’t count.


Rule 3

Reward yourself every time you get rejected to keep you motivated to go on. For example, play video games when you get home or go to the sport practice if you are play any sport.


And that’s it, after a month you will be free from the fear of getting rejected and you will live your life the way you want without feeling sad anytime you get rejected on something.
